EXPLORE GILLITTS

A Village Of Valleys,
Gorges & Weekend Markets

Maison Norelle sits at the heart of the Upper Highway — a hillside pocket of KwaZulu-Natal known for its forest trails, farm stalls, and long weekend mornings at the market. Here are a few of our favourite spots nearby.

Nature & Conservation

Springside Nature Reserve

A living classroom in conservation, with walking trails through indigenous forest and streams — a peaceful escape just minutes from your suite.

Saturday Mornings

Shongweni Farmers & Craft Market

One of KwaZulu-Natal's best-loved weekend markets, drawing around 8,000 visitors every Saturday for local produce, craft, and food stalls.

Trails & Adventure

Giba Gorge Nature Reserve

KwaZulu-Natal's premier eco-sport destination, with roughly 35km of trails winding through indigenous bush — ideal for hiking and mountain biking.
