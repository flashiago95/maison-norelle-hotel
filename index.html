Get to Know Your Hillside Sanctuary

Perched in the verdant Upper Highway corridor, Maison Norelle is more than a boutique hotel — it is a sanctuary where grace meets contemporary sophistication, just 30 minutes from Durban.

Hillside Highlights

Landscapes, Legacies & Learning

The Upper Highway corridor holds some of KwaZulu-Natal's most storied natural and cultural landmarks — all within easy reach of Maison Norelle.

Springside Nature Reserve

21 hectares preserving one of the last remaining fragments of mistbelt grassland in the Durban area, with over 150 recorded bird species.

Read More

Shongweni Farmers & Craft Market

From under 20 vendors in the late 1990s to over 200 traders today, welcoming an average of 8,000 visitors every Saturday.

Read More

Giba Gorge Nature Reserve

A 72-hectare precinct with 35km of purpose-built single-track mountain biking trails — a premier eco-sport destination.

Culinary & Artisan Delights

Taste the culture of the hills

Culinary exploration in Gillitts and Hillcrest is as much about culture as it is about taste. Takami Sushi offers a refined Japanese experience, La Verdure Real Food Café emphasizes farm-to-table dining, and The Pot and Kettle has paired panoramic views with authentic South African comfort food since the early 1980s.

Artisan studios and boutique shops abound, producing pottery, textiles, and hand-crafted gifts — each a tangible connection to the region's heritage.

In every dish lies a story; in every craft, a heritage. Together they are the art of living.

An Insider's Weekend

A weekend at Maison Norelle

Time slows and the senses awaken. Every corner invites pause and reflection — a stay here becomes less about visiting, and more about belonging.

Friday Night

Arrive at Maison Norelle, immerse yourself in the curated interiors, garden terraces, and gentle ambiance — a perfect prelude to the weekend.

Saturday Morning

Explore Springside Nature Reserve or meander through the bustling Shongweni Farmers & Craft Market, savoring artisanal delicacies along the way.

Saturday Afternoon

Return to the hotel for reflective leisure — a book in the lounge or tea in the gardens — then a scenic drive through the Valley of a Thousand Hills.

Sunday

For those seeking adventure, mountain bike through Giba Gorge or enjoy wildlife observation at Alverstone Park, concluding with a farewell brunch.
