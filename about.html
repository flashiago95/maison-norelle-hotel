OUR STORY

A Hillside Haven,
30 Minutes From Durban

Perched in the verdant Upper Highway corridor, Maison Norelle is more than a boutique guest house — it is a sanctuary where grace meets contemporary sophistication. Nestled in Gillitts, Hillcrest, our four suites were designed for guests who want the comfort of a private home with the polish of a proper stay.

Every detail has been curated to provide guests with an experience that lingers long after check-out — from luxurious interiors to garden terraces that invite reflection. Here, you are not merely visiting; you are stepping into the art of grace living.
