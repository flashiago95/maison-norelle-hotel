Suite Overview

Our most spacious suite, the Presidential Suite is a true home away from home. A separate lounge with its own smart TV joins the bedroom's second screen, while a full self-catering kitchen and bar fridge give guests complete independence. Air conditioning keeps the suite comfortable year-round, and the generous floor plan makes this the top choice for guests staying a while, or those who simply want to arrive and feel instantly at ease.
